Why you should focus on your skill ??

Understanding your skills, interests, values, and personality is the first step toward choosing a career path. Learning what you enjoy, what you're good at, and what you value most in your life or work environment is necessary before you embark on exploring possible careers.

A degree can just earn the job, but it cannot help to grow further without the skill. - It is skill which attracts the employers, clients and management which lifts or drops the person. Without the skill, the person would not be able to catch hold the interest of their superiors and attain success.
